# `marketing-influencer`

## Overview

Builds influencer and creator partnership strategies, outreach, and campaign plans.

## When to use it

Use `/marketing-influencer` when you need:
- creator discovery criteria
- outreach sequences
- influencer campaign planning
- ambassador or UGC program design

## Inputs to prepare

- audience and product fit
- budget and compensation model
- creator profile criteria
- campaign objective
- brand safety constraints

## Deliverables

- influencer shortlists
- outreach plans
- campaign structures
- partnership recommendations

## Output locations

```text
brands/{brand-slug}/campaigns/influencer/
```

## Related skills

- `marketing-social`
- `marketing-pr`
- `marketing-launch`
- `marketing-video`

## Sample prompts

```text
/marketing-influencer
Build an influencer strategy for our product.
```

```text
/marketing-influencer
For Acorn Legal, identify the best creator profile types for trust-building among small law firm owners.
```

```text
/marketing-influencer
Use our launch campaign and audience profile to plan a micro-influencer push.
```
