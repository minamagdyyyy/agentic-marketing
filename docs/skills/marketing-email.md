# `marketing-email`

## Overview

Creates email strategy and assets across lifecycle, nurture, outbound, launch, and retention.

## When to use it

Use `/marketing-email` when you need:
- welcome or nurture sequences
- newsletters
- launch emails
- win-back or retention emails
- cold outbound sequences

## Inputs to prepare

- audience segment
- goal and offer
- funnel stage
- objections and proof
- sending context or ESP constraints

## Deliverables

- sequences
- templates
- campaign plans
- automation recommendations

## Output locations

```text
brands/{brand-slug}/content/email/
```

## Related skills

- `marketing-content`
- `marketing-retention`
- `marketing-sales`
- `marketing-launch`

## Sample prompts

```text
/marketing-email
Write a 5-email welcome sequence.
```

```text
/marketing-email
For Acorn Legal, create a 4-email nurture sequence for firms that requested a demo but did not book a call.
```

```text
/marketing-email
Use our product marketing context and launch plan to draft the announcement emails for our new feature release.
```
