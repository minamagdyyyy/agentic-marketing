# `marketing-guerrilla`

## Overview

Designs unconventional, high-leverage campaigns for attention and growth.

## When to use it

Use `/marketing-guerrilla` when you need:
- low-budget attention plays
- unusual campaign ideas
- scrappy growth experiments
- creative disruption in crowded channels

## Inputs to prepare

- target audience context
- constraints and budget
- risk tolerance
- campaign objective
- any brand or legal limits

## Deliverables

- concept ideas
- experiment plans
- execution outlines

## Output locations

```text
brands/{brand-slug}/campaigns/guerrilla/
```

## Related skills

- `marketing-pr`
- `marketing-social`
- `marketing-launch`
- `marketing-community`

## Sample prompts

```text
/marketing-guerrilla
Give us three low-budget guerrilla campaign concepts.
```

```text
/marketing-guerrilla
For Acorn Legal, design an unconventional awareness campaign that reaches small law firm owners without relying on paid ads.
```

```text
/marketing-guerrilla
Use our positioning and budget constraints to create one bold launch stunt plus a safer backup version.
```
