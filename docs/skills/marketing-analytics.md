# `marketing-analytics`

## Overview

Handles measurement, tracking, reporting, and performance analysis.

## When to use it

Use `/marketing-analytics` when you need:
- tracking plans
- event naming or UTM standards
- dashboard and reporting structure
- attribution guidance
- funnel or cohort analysis

## Inputs to prepare

- active brand
- KPI definitions
- channel mix
- current analytics tools
- known tracking gaps

## Deliverables

- tracking plans
- audits
- reporting docs
- measurement recommendations

## Output locations

```text
brands/{brand-slug}/analytics/
```

Often including tracking, reports, and audit subfolders.

## Related skills

- `marketing-sostac`
- `marketing-cro`
- `marketing-paid-ads`
- `marketing-retention`
- `marketing-seo`

```text
/marketing-analytics
Audit our current marketing tracking setup.
```

```text
/marketing-analytics
Create a reporting framework for paid ads, SEO, lifecycle email, and sign-up conversion.
```

```text

Use our SOSTAC control phase and build a practical KPI dashboard spec.
```
