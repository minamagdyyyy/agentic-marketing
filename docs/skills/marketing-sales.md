# `marketing-sales`

## Overview

Creates sales enablement assets and messaging systems for the revenue team.

## When to use it

Use `/marketing-sales` when you need:
- sales decks
- one-pagers
- objection handling docs
- demo scripts
- ROI messaging
- champion kits

## Inputs to prepare

- ICP and segment details
- common objections
- sales stage context
- competitor or pricing pressure
- product proof and outcomes

## Deliverables

- sales assets
- enablement documents
- objection-handling frameworks
- library organization

## Output locations

```text
brands/{brand-slug}/campaigns/sales/
```

## Related skills

- `marketing-email`
- `marketing-pricing`
- `marketing-cro`
- `product-marketing-context`

## Sample prompts

```text
/marketing-sales
Create a one-pager for our product.
```

```text
/marketing-sales
For Acorn Legal, build objection-handling and demo messaging for small law firm owners.
```

```text
/marketing-sales
Use our positioning, proof points, and pricing strategy to draft a sales deck outline.
```
