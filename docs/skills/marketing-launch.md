# `marketing-launch`

## Overview

Coordinates product, feature, or company launches across channels.

## When to use it

Use `/marketing-launch` when you need:
- launch strategy
- announcement sequencing
- launch-day planning
- Product Hunt support
- launch content coordination

## Inputs to prepare

- launch date or window
- audience
- product or feature details
- assets and proof
- KPI targets

## Deliverables

- launch strategy
- launch brief
- schedule
- launch content pack
- performance review structure

## Output locations

```text
brands/{brand-slug}/campaigns/launch/
```

## Related skills

- `marketing-pr`
- `marketing-email`
- `marketing-social`
- `marketing-paid-ads`
- `marketing-sales`

## Sample prompts

```text
/marketing-launch
Create a launch plan for our new feature.
```

```text
/marketing-launch
For Northstar AI, build a 30-day go-to-market plan for our recruiting scorecard release.
```

```text
/marketing-launch
Use our completed SOSTAC plan to create the cross-channel launch brief, then identify which assets should be handed off to email, social, PR, and paid ads.
```
