# `marketing-seo`

## Overview

Covers technical SEO, content SEO, local SEO, link building, and AI search optimization.

## When to use it

Use `/marketing-seo` when you need:
- an SEO audit
- keyword research
- content briefs for search
- schema ideas
- local SEO recommendations
- GEO or AI Overview readiness work

## Inputs to prepare

- active brand
- site or page scope
- target topics or keywords
- competitors
- any existing analytics or ranking data

## Deliverables

- SEO audits
- keyword research docs
- monthly action plans
- content briefs
- schema files

## Output locations

```text
brands/{brand-slug}/content/seo/
```

Including common subfolders such as:
- `content-briefs/`
- `schema/`
- `link-building/`
- `local-seo/`

## Related skills

- `marketing-content`
- `marketing-analytics`
- `marketing-cro`
- `marketing-pr`

## Sample prompts

```text
/marketing-seo
Run a technical and content SEO audit.
```

```text
/marketing-seo
For Northstar AI, build keyword research around recruiting automation, interview notes, and candidate scorecards.
```

```text
/marketing-seo
Use our SOSTAC plan and product marketing context to create three high-intent SEO briefs.
```
