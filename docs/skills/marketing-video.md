# `marketing-video`

## Overview

Handles short-form, YouTube, live streaming, ad scripts, and video production planning.

## When to use it

Use `/marketing-video` when you need:
- short-form video ideas or scripts
- a YouTube strategy
- video briefs or storyboards
- live-stream planning
- ad scripts for video campaigns

## Inputs to prepare

- target platform
- audience and message
- CTA or offer
- production constraints
- brand voice and objections

## Deliverables

- scripts
- calendars
- YouTube strategy docs
- briefs
- storyboards
- ad scripts

## Output locations

```text
brands/{brand-slug}/content/video/
```

Common subfolders:
- `scripts/`
- `briefs/`
- `storyboards/`
- `ad-scripts/`
- `performance/`

- `marketing-social`

```text

Create five short-form video concepts for our product.
```
