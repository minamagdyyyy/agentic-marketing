# `marketing-psychology`

## Overview

Applies behavioral science and persuasion patterns to messaging, offers, and UX.

## When to use it

Use `/marketing-psychology` when you need:
- stronger persuasion in copy
- framing help for offers or pages
- objection-handling ideas
- bias-aware review of messaging and UX

## Inputs to prepare

- target audience
- current copy or flow
- desired action
- known objections
- brand voice constraints

## Deliverables

- persuasion recommendations
- annotated messaging guidance
- framing and offer suggestions

## Output locations

This skill is often advisory and may write into the active deliverable rather than a single dedicated folder.

## Related skills

- `marketing-cro`
- `marketing-email`
- `marketing-paid-ads`
- `marketing-sales`
- `marketing-content`

## Sample prompts

```text
/marketing-psychology
Review our homepage messaging and suggest stronger persuasion patterns.
```

```text
/marketing-psychology
Use our audience objections to improve the framing of our webinar signup page.
```

```text
/marketing-psychology
Audit this launch copy for weak framing, trust gaps, and missed behavioral triggers.
```
